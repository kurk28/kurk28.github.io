I Work Best

I understand the reason why we are doing a task and the task should not be abstracted.

I know where all tools and documentations for my work at.

I work on one task at the moment.

I use dual monitors to stay efficient. A structured workspace helps me focus. They don't need to be big, for instance, right now I'm working with MacBook and additional 16" monitor from Arzopa.

When working, I mute notifications and listen to focus-enhancing sounds (Pink Noise, Brown Noise, 40 Hz Gamma Waves). If I have headphones on, I’m in deep work mode, just message me, and I’ll respond soon.

I prefer planned meetings to ensure focused discussions, but I’m always open to urgent matters when needed.

I work best with short and focused meetings rather than long discussions.

I have a pour sense of time. Small tasks with deadlines work great for me.

I'm a visual thinker. If we can draw our thoughts or ideas, let's do it! I use Excalidraw
